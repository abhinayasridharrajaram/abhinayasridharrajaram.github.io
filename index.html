About Me

I am a lawyer turned Analyst adept at going beneath the surface of a problem, finding questions at its heart, and distilling them into a very clear set of hypotheses that can be tested.


I articulate, facilitate & communicate easily with other stakeholders on regulations, requirements & precipitate more efficient and transparent data processes with the singular goal of developing data driven/informed culture in business.

Motivation

1) I employ state of the-art modeling techniques in audit to identify risks and provide significantly improved opportunities for auditors to explore their intuition.

2) I offer forward looking solutions in the Audit department including but not limited to financial crime prevention.

3) I do this by the use of descriptive statistics, hypothesis testing and machine learning in audits to give auditors a greater ability to consider internal systematic relationships and external environmental forces.
